1. Setup
Put your phone in the middle of the board so that each player can access the biomes values of their expeditions.
2. Biomes
2
0
In each biome, there are 2 values:
● Bottom right : The value of your biomes.
● Top left: Your opponent's value for the same biomes on the same expedition.
The value in yellow is the highest.
3. Biomes Values
2
0
You can use the arrows around the biomes to increase or decrease their values by 1
For example, if one of your characters is removed from the board, you can manually decrease the values of your biomes to remove the character's stats.
4. Boosts
On each of your shipments you have access to these buttons:
● +1 increases all biomes by 1
● -1 decreases all biomes by 1
5. Add a character
When you play a new character, you can click on this button on the desired expedition side.
A window opens where you can choose the values for each of your character's biomes. Once confirmed, these values will be added to your current biomes.
6. Actives biomes
2
0
When one of your tokens moves forward, the exploration card it moves onto defines the new biomes. Biomes that are not on this card can be clicked to disable them.
7. Tokens indications
On the 2 expeditions, you have real-time status of the corresponding tokens.
● If no one is advancing, there are no arrows.
● If you should advance, the arrow on your side will be visible, and the same for your opponent.
● If you both have to advance, both arrows are visible.
This system takes into consideration the state of the biomes.
8. Settings
You can click on this button in the center of the screen to open the settings.
You can choose your preferred theme. You can also enable a timer, a solo mode and zoom based on your preferences.
